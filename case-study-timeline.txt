Step 01 - Intake
User reports inability to log in Monday morning. Ticket opened in Jira, priority set to High, assigned to Tier 1 support.

Step 02 - Scope Check
Confirmed issue is isolated to one user. Checked account status — no system-wide outage reported.

Step 03 - Root Cause
Password expired over the weekend. Account locked after multiple failed login attempts by the user.

Step 04 - Resolution
Reset password in Active Directory, unlocked account, enforced password change on next login. Confirmed with user via phone.

Step 05 - Closure
User confirmed successful login. Ticket updated with resolution notes and closed in Jira. Recommended enabling password expiry email notifications to prevent recurrence.
